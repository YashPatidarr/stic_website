Mechanical Department

Mechatronics

Intro Duction to Mechatronics.

The interdisciplinary fraternity of STIC is a community of candidates who gain pleasure in tinkering with machines which have the potential to survive without human assistance. Here you will come across building machines which are capable of performing simple assigned tasks by making use of mechanisms algorithms whilst keeping in mind the concept of its mechanical feasibility and endurance.

DIVISIONS


To promote technical hobby hands on activities in the institute, this club would cherish every project which requires prerequisites from each sphere of engineering :

  • Computing
  • Control system
  • Digital control system
  • Control electronics
  • Electronic system
  • Electromechanics
  • Mechanical engineering
  • Design and analysis (CAD)
